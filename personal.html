Conferences are a wonderful excuse for me to travel. I love both culture and nature, and the contrast between them. One day I am watching an opera at the Vienna Opera House, and the next I am hunting piranhas in the middle of the Amazon rainforest. Whenever I land somewhere new, I take the chance to explore the amazing places of that country and bring a few photos back.
