Fatemeh Pesaran zadeh

Hello! I am a 2nd year Master's student at Seoul National University, Computer Science department, Vision & learning lab, advised by Gunhee Kim. My research interests lie in solving difficult problems in Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and optimizing them using reinforcement learning. Specifically, I have worked on using automatic reward to optimize LLMs for the chart generation task. I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science from Seoul National University in 2022.
